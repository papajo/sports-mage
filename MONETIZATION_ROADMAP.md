## ðŸ’° Monetization Strategies

### 1. Primary Revenue Streams

#### A. Commission-Based Betting (Primary)
- **Model**: Take 5-10% commission on all bets (industry standard)
- **Implementation**: 
  - Calculate commission on winning bets
  - Built into odds calculation
  - Transparent fee structure
- **Revenue Potential**: High (scales with user base)

#### B. Subscription Tiers
- **Free Tier**: 
  - Limited betting markets
  - Basic odds display
  - Standard bet limits ($1-$100)
  - Basic statistics
  
- **Premium Tier ($9.99/month)**:
  - All betting markets
  - Advanced statistics and analytics
  - Higher bet limits ($1-$1,000)
  - Live streaming integration
  - Expert picks and insights
  - Early access to new features
  
- **Pro Tier ($29.99/month)**:
  - Everything in Premium
  - Unlimited bet limits
  - Real-time odds alerts
  - Advanced betting tools (parlay builder, bet calculator)
  - API access for developers
  - White-label options
  - Priority customer support

#### C. In-App Purchases
- **Betting Credits**: Users can purchase credits at a premium (5-10% markup)
- **Premium Features**: One-time purchases for specific tools
- **Virtual Currency**: Create "SportsMage Coins" for in-app transactions

#### D. Advertising Revenue
- **Display Ads**: Banner ads for sports-related products
- **Sponsored Content**: Featured odds from specific bookmakers
- **Affiliate Marketing**: Commission from referring users to other services

#### E. Data Licensing
- **API Access**: Sell access to betting odds and statistics API
- **White-Label Solution**: License the platform to other operators
- **Data Analytics**: Sell aggregated betting trends and insights

#### F. Premium Features (A La Carte)
- **Expert Picks**: $4.99/week for professional handicapper picks
- **Live Streaming**: $2.99/game for premium live streams
- **Advanced Analytics**: $9.99/month for detailed team/player analytics
- **Betting Tools**: One-time purchase for advanced calculators
